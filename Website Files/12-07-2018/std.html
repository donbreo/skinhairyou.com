STD/HIV

Unsafe sex can often lead to some troublesome diseases, more widely known as Sexually Transmitted Diseases (STDs) or even HIV in worst case scenarios. These diseases can cause various distressing symptoms to patients which might result in a bad skin or long-lasting effects on your skin or hair. Some of the common symptoms include itching or redness in the genital area, rashes or ulcers in the genital area and others. Especially in the case of HIV, skin conditions like rashes, lesions or sores can be the early signs of the disease in the body. These can be either general dermatitis, bacterial or viral infections or skin tumours. Such conditions require immediate attention and counselling and thus, at Skin, Hair & You, we offer end to end treatment solutions for any kind of skin problems associated with these diseases. We also provide HIV screening and sexual dysfunctional counselling at affordable costs.
