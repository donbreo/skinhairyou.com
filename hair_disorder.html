Services

Hair Disorders

SK&U offers natural hair restoration treatments for male and female pattern hair loss. Our range of services includes mesotherapy, unwanted hair reduction by laser, dermatoscopic analysis and platelet rich plasma (PRP). While various hair loss conditions can affect your scalp, it can result in lower self-esteem and confidence, affecting your daily life to a great extent. The right choice of therapy and treatment is largely based on the type of hair disorder, patient’s age, gender and extent of the disease. Whether you’re suffering from an autoimmune disorder like alopecia areata or have significant hair loss due to thyroid or other medical conditions, we can help you regain your hair with effective treatment solutions. Trust our experts to evaluate and treat your hair loss problems with only the best options.
