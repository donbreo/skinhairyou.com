Hair Transplant (FUE)

FUE is one of the most modern and scientific technique for hair restoration. It is a scarless, stitch less and painless procedure in which grafted follicular units are transplanted in the affected area. A hair transplant surgeon normally repeats the treatment until adequate follicular units are harvested on the scalp. You can opt for this innovative treatment to cure graying and thinning hair, severe dandruff, bald patches on the scalp (alopecia areata) and also a flaky scalp. At Skin, Hair & You, we have the experts who leverage their impeccable knowledge and experience to execute this process with ultimate ease for best results. Enjoy an improved hair texture, get rid of your dry, damaged or frizzy hair and enhance your hair volume and density. It is our endeavour to provide you with the most natural hairline that will help you regain your confidence and love for your hair!
