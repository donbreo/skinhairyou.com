About us

Established in the year 2012, Skin, Hair & You is a recognised clinic for skin care, cosmetic and hair services.

We specialize in a range of result-driven aesthetic treatments that are suitable for all ages and skin types. Our key areas of expertise include Dermatology, Hair transplant, Cosmetology, Anti-Ageing, and Laser. All our services are provided in a professional and service-oriented environment, backed by ethical practices, modern technology and certified experts which give us the benefit to help you look your best and always feel fantastic about yourself! With two state-of-the-art clinics in Mumbai and Pune, we ensure long-lasting results for our clients with minimum side effects and personalized treatments for better skin and hair. We understand that everyone desires beautiful skin and hair, irrespective of their age and gender and thus, deliver your desired beauty in a way that enhances and transforms your life.
